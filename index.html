The Bosses

They are very well designed and unique with there're own set of abilites. They increase health based on the amount of players there are and they increase health and damage the further along you get in the game

The Characters

The charcters all have unique magic that unlockes at a certain level. They also unlock combos through levels. You can get new characters by beating a the game with a certain character or by doing colloseums around the map. also some levels on the map reward characters.

Level Design

The Levels are all different based on which section of the game you are at and they are all very detailed with lots of enemies throught. After you beat the game you unlock insane mode which provides a much greater challenge than the base game. Enemies have increased health and damage which includes bosses. You also unlock new characters through this mode.
